Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Monday through Saturday, and from 12:00 PM to 9:00 PM on Sundays. Delivery times may vary during holidays or special events.

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes! We have several vegetarian and vegan options, including our popular Veggie Burger, Falafel Shawarma, and Vegetable Jollof Rice. Please inform our staff about any dietary restrictions when ordering.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your order free of charge up to 30 minutes after placing it. After that time, a cancellation fee may apply. For catering orders, we require at least 48 hours notice for cancellations.

Do you offer catering for large events?

Absolutely! We cater events of all sizes, from small office meetings to large weddings and parties. Contact our catering team at catering@tastybites.com or call (123) 456-7891 to discuss your needs.

How can I provide feedback about my experience?

We welcome all feedback! You can use the contact form on this page, email us at feedback@tastybites.com, or leave a review on our social media pages. We read every piece of feedback and use it to improve our service.
